FAQ

Clear answers before money moves.

Does Akara hold my money?

No. Akara does not hold, move, escrow, remit, convert, or receive user funds. Users pay each other directly.

Do I need verification?

Yes. Verification is required before creating listings, opening trades, adding payout accounts, or completing exchanges.

What currencies are supported?

Akara launches with NGN, RWF, GHS, KES, and XAF.

What if something goes wrong?

Raise a dispute. Akara reviews receipts, payout details, KYC records, chats, and admin notes.
